Our Story

Three generations of the Henderson family have worked this land since 1987. What began as a small fruit stand has grown into a thriving organic farm, but our commitment to sustainable practices and quality produce has never wavered.

We believe in farming that works with nature, not against it. Our berries are grown without synthetic pesticides, and we use integrated pest management and crop rotation to maintain healthy soil. Every strawberry you buy from us carries not just flavor, but a story of care and dedication.

Our Farming Practices

Organic Certification

Certified USDA Organic since 2005. No synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or fertilizers used in our fields.

Water Conservation

Drip irrigation and rainwater collection systems reduce water usage by 40% compared to conventional farms.

Pest Management

Beneficial insects and companion planting create a balanced ecosystem that naturally controls pests.
