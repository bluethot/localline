Our Story

Three generations of the Henderson family have worked this land since 1987. What began as a small fruit stand has grown into a thriving organic farm, but our commitment to sustainable practices and quality produce has never wavered.

We believe in farming that works with nature, not against it. Our berries are grown without synthetic pesticides, and we use integrated pest management and crop rotation to maintain healthy soil. Every strawberry you buy from us carries not just flavor, but a story of care and dedication.

Our Journey

1987 - Humble Beginnings

The Henderson family purchases 20 acres in Salinas Valley and plants their first strawberry fields.

1995 - Transition to Organic

We begin transitioning our farming practices to organic methods, completing the process in 2000.

2005 - USDA Certification

After years of hard work, we receive official USDA Organic certification.

2012 - Farm Stand Opens

We open our farm stand to the public, selling directly to our community.

2020 - Next Generation

The third generation of Hendersons joins the farm, bringing new ideas and energy.

Our Farming Practices

Organic Certification

Certified USDA Organic since 2005. No synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or fertilizers used in our fields.

Water Conservation

Drip irrigation and rainwater collection systems reduce water usage by 40% compared to conventional farms.

Pest Management

Beneficial insects and companion planting create a balanced ecosystem that naturally controls pests.

Soil Health

We practice crop rotation, cover cropping, and compost application to maintain fertile, living soil.

Renewable Energy

Our farm runs on 100% renewable energy from solar panels and a small wind turbine.

Fair Labor

We pay living wages and provide benefits to all our workers year-round.
