How LocalharvestChain Works

Transparency at every step of your food's journey

1. Farmers Register

Farmers create verified profiles and list their products with complete growing details.

2. Products Get Digital IDs

Each product batch receives a unique NFT with immutable records of its origin and journey.

3. Consumers Shop & Verify

Scan QR codes to see the complete history before purchase.
